Mission

In life Mother Teresa of Calcutta (1910-1997)was a household name,the most famous missionary of modern times.Her selfless service to the poor,sick and dying was the living embodiment of Christian charity,piety and love.Mother Teresa's tireless missionary work was known to millions,believers and non-believers alike.As a missionary Mother Teresa was a great believer in the cumulative effects of doing God's work in the world.
