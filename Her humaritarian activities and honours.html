Her humaritarian activities and honours
   
   
   
   

For the selfless,dedicated and motherly activities,she received 'Padmashree' from the Government of india in 1962.She also received a fashionable car as a token of gift from Pop paul VI and later sold it to get money for a refuge of the lepers.She founded 'Sishu Bhaban' in Entally near sealdah for the shelter of the orphans.In 1965 mother set up her working office in Venezuella.In 1979 Mother was awarded the Nobel prize for the universal motherhood.The highest title of India Bharat Ratna was conferred on her 1980.In 1985 she accepted the medal of freedom at white house,U.S.Calcutta is justly proud of being the ever of peaceful abode of two Nobel Laureates-Rabindra Tagore and Mother Teresa.Mother's inspiring message-'To serve the poor is to serve God,who is not dead but alive and full of love'.
