Her early life

Mother Teresa's real name was Agnes Gonxhas Bojajazhim.She was born at Scopji in Yugoslavia on 27th August in 1910.Just at the age of seven she lost her father.Her mother was apious lady.Agnes joined the convent of Loreto in Dublin,Ireland,in 1928 when she was only eighteen.She came in close contact with the jesuit fathers in Calcutta and was profoundly inspired by the works of charity.While in Calcutta she became a teacher of Geography in Loreto school,Darjeeling.In 1931 she joined st.Mary's school and later became a teacher of Loreto Convent for eighteen years in Calcutta.
