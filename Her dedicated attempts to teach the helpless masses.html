Mother Teresa




Her dedicated attempts to teach the helpless masses
   
   
   
   
During this time Mother rendered her best services to the inmates of Motijheel Basti.The year 1946 opened a new vista for her.On her way to Darjeeling she heard the heavenly voice of Jesus to dedicate herself in the service of the poorest of the poor.Being inspired,she put on a blue boarded white saree and became Mother Teresa.She then undertook the nurshing training in Patna and accepted the citizenship of india.In Motijheel area she set up aschool for orphans.In 1950 she founded the Missionaries of charity recognised by the Pope of Rome.Her Humanitarian activities spread far and near.She set up 'Nirmal Hriday' in kalighat and 'Nirmala kennedy centre' in Dum Dum.
